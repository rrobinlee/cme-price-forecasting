# CME Seat and Commodity Price Forecasting

In this repo, I forecast CME monthly seat prices in 2013 using: (1) linear regression, (2) linear regression with ARMA errors, (3) Holt-Winters ETS, (4) ARIMA, (5) SARIMA, (6) ARFIMA, and (7) ARMA-GARCH. I also forecast 2 commodities (CL & GC) using a Bayesian Structural Time Series (BSTS), with macroeconomic (CPI) and index (SPX) exogenous variables.

Data consists of prices for which CME seats sold, the date of the sale, and the monthly trading volume for different CME products from January 2001 to December 2012. 


*Completed using R in 2025 at The University of Chicago, ADSP 31006; currently reworking in Python*
