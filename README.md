# CME Seat and Commodities Price Forecasting

In this repo, I forecast the CME monthly seat price in 2013 using the following statistical models: (1) linear regression, (2) linear regression with ARMA errors, (3) Holt-Winters ETS, (4) ARIMA, (5) SARIMA, (6) ARFIMA, and (7) ARMA-GARCH. Data consists of prices for which CME seats sold, the date of the sale, and the monthly trading volume for different CME products from January 2001 to December 2012. I also forecast 2 commodities (CL & GC) using a Bayesian Structural Time Series (BSTS), with macroeconomic (CPI) and index (SPX) exogenous variables.

*Completed in R as part of ADSP 31006 coursework at The University of Chicago, currently translating into Python.*
